Past Events

Relive past Roblox events and seasonal celebrations.

The Classic Event (May 23-28, 2024)

The Classic was an event themed around classic or old Roblox. It featured portals to user-created experiences, with a central hub experience. Users could complete challenges across fifteen participating experiences to unlock limited-time event-related avatar items.

Egg Hunt 2018: The Great Yolktales

In this event, players embarked on an egg-citing adventure across various worlds to find hidden eggs, each with unique designs and challenges.

Halloween Event 2019: Hallow's Eve (Sinister Swamp)

Players explored the Sinister Swamp, completing spooky quests and collecting exclusive Halloween-themed items.

Ready Player Two Event (2020)

Based on the "Ready Player Two" novel, this event had players solving intricate puzzles across different games to earn exclusive virtual items.

Metaverse Champions (April 15 - May 20, 2021)

A successor to the Egg Hunt events, players collected mystery boxes in various games to support their favorite champions and unlock rewards.
